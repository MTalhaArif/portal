Important Notice for All Students

All students who have successfully obtained their Turkish visa and are planning to travel to Türkiye must not purchase their flight tickets before receiving the official  confirmation email from the university.

After receiving your visa, please send us an email request so we can arrange the university confirmation email.

Important: Immigration authorities (FIA) may ask to see this university confirmation email at the airport. Students without it may face travel difficulties.

*Please follow this order:*

 Obtain your visa

Send us your email request.

Receive the university confirmation email.

Purchase your flight ticket.

Travel to Türkiye safely.


Thank you for your cooperation.
